"Untitled" (Portrait of My Opened Tabs)

is a digital collection by Mintie, currently displayed on this Tabs Manager website, a personal archive that reflects the common phenomenon of tab hoarding. In today’s fast-paced digital world, we all find ourselves accumulating dozens (or even hundreds) of open tabs—each one representing a task, interest, or curiosity that we may or may not return to. This collection captures the reality of digital life: the constant juggling of information, entertainment, and productivity, often with no clear end in sight. As we navigate the internet, our browsers become cluttered with tabs we leave open, waiting for the right moment to be dealt with.

This collection is part of the Web to Print class at California College of the Arts (CCA) for Fall 2024.
